Forms: Level Five Heading

Generally speaking, it's best not to get too wild with styling form elements. Users have some expectations for what these interactive controls should look like on their OS and browser, and deviating too far from that expected styling makes forms harder to use. Some form elements are also notoriously difficult to style consistently across browsers and platforms so sometimes it's not to try.
